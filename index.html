About

ELESARO PLATFORM

Elesaro aims to disrupt the traditional crowdfunding sector by leveraging on blockchain technology
to create a borderless system where individuals around the world can create, donate and invest in a project while gaining an ROI and Rewards with our Elf Coin.

Elesaro is being built for
Investors, Entrepreneurs and Creatives.

With the Elf coin, crypto enthusiast and investors an invest in well curated and promising startups while gaining an Equity in return. Elf coin holders can also stake their coin to for ROI over a period of time. The donation/pledge option is also made available for Elf coin holders to donate to a creative campaign or causes they are passionate about and be rewarded.

With Elesaro, Startups ,Founders and Entrepreneurs can crowdfund for their businesses while building a community around their product. Elesaro curates and approves only exciting projects with high chances of being valuable.

Creators including, film makers, digital & comic artists, musicians and authors can crowdfund for their projects by offering exciting rewards to potential backers.

Problems & Solutions

90% of businesses fail – majority of these ventures has cited “Lack of funding as the major reason why it failed”
“Lack of funding as the major reason why it failed”

Problems

Lack of funds has been a lingering issue for entrepreneurs and creative. A regular Reward-based crowdfunding platform charges a higher a fee on the amount raised from their platform (This usually totals 7% - 10% of the fee raised by donor) while limiting users from certain region from raising campaigns on their platform.

Even Regular equity crowdfunding platforms limits retail investors from investing in an early stage startup hence restricting them from the same kind of opportunity presented to the so called accredited investors.

Solutions

With Elesaro leveraging on blockchain technology, crypto enthusiast and investors can invest in well curated startup and companies around the world using ELF COIN while gaining Equity in Return. Users can also donate to their favorite creative campaigns and gain fantastic reward including ELF COIN.

The campaign commission fee is extremely low compared to the traditional system. The New system is borderless.
